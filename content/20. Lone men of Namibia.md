There are super cool stone sculptures placed around the region. These creative works-of-art are perfect for the terrain. They are all postured differently and not always that easy to spot. 
They are shrouded in mystery regarding their locations and who the artist is.

I've demystified their whereabouts by sourcing their locations and marking their locations in the vicinity of our travels. One still has to spot them considering how well they blend in. 

I think we will pass about 8 of the twenty something men. 
If you want to see the locations I've marked, refer to the map and make sure the "StoneMenEnRoute" layer of the map is turned on. You should see several "Yellow men" locations on the map.
